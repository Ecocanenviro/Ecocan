Who We Are

Where science meets
stewardship

"We believe that responsible industry and a healthy environment are not opposites — they are partners."

Eco-Can Environmental is a Canadian environmental consulting firm with years of professional experience supporting industrial, municipal, and Indigenous clients across western and northern Canada.

From our roots in British Columbia, we have grown to serve clients across the provinces — delivering practical, cost-effective solutions that meet the highest environmental and regulatory standards.

Science-First Methodology

Decisions grounded in peer-reviewed methods, data, and field expertise

Indigenous Community Engagement

Respectful, meaningful partnerships with First Nations across all project areas

Transparent Reporting

Clear, defensible documentation that stands up to regulatory and public scrutiny
